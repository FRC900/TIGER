Intro to Strategy

The following section is a curriculum for teaching strategy utilizing the above presentation and modified rules.

Strategy is the first step for build season. It is defined as a careful plan or method and is used to evaluate the rules to come up with a game plan for the season.

Strategy is important because it creates a guideline for build season. It gives you a way to mathamatically decide what to prioritize as opposed to what you think might be best. Once you have a strategy, you can prioritize your prototyping and coding efforts to what is most important for your team.
