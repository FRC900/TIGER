Helpful Resources

There are many resources available to FRC teams. Here is a list of some of them that don't fit into any of the other categories.

General Links

The Blue Alliance

A great resource for looking at past games, robot designs, and matches! Also good for events currently happening and insights.

FRC Q&A

Good resource for questions about the game rules and robot rules during build and competition season. Read the rules before you ask questions though.

Twitch

Great for live videos of matches happening (TBA uses twitch) Channels of note: Firstinspires, First_North_Carolina, and FirstUpdatesNow

Zebracorn Labs

All of the whitepapers our team has written.

MidnightU

Many general resources for all aspects of FRC teams

Chief Delphi

A place to get questions answered (unofficially), and see what other teams are doing! Take this resource with a grain of salt as it can get contentious and people can be wrong.

YouTube

Good for visual interpretation of robots in action (better for older videos)

Timesheet

Github repository for our hours tracking system

FIRST Wiki

A wiki about FRC teams

NC Photos

Danny Levenson is the official photographer for NC and posts his photos of events here.
